Welcome to Quizzle

Rules

    • Try to answer as many questions as possible under the shortest amount of time.
    • Try not to fail any question as this results in a negative mark.

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Speed Matters: Answer questions quickly to maximize your score. Remember, time is of the essence!
  • Penalty for Timeout: If you let the timer run out, points will be deducted from your score. Stay alert!
  • Deduction for Incorrect Answers: Incorrect responses result in score deductions. Accuracy is key!
  • Score Calculation: Your score is determined by the speed and accuracy of your answers. Strive for both!
